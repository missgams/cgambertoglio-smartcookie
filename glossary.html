TERM MEANING USES DIFFICULTY
blind baking the process of baking the pastry shell separately from the filling making pies and tarts medium
blooming the process of soaking gelatin sheets or gelatin powder in cold water before incorporating to thicken things up easy
cocoa butter the edible cocoa fat extracted from cocoa beans desserts and cosmetics easy
crumb coat the process of applying a thin layer of frosting to a cake to catch loose crumbs to ensure the final coat is nice and smooth medium
fondant a coating for cakes made from powdered sugar, gelatin, water, glucose, and glycerin provides a very smooth outer coat difficult
génoise a classic French cake that has a light, airy texture developed without the aid of leavening agents a great base for cream filled cakes medium
macaron a cookie made from sugar, egg whites, and ground almonds delicious treat for your gluten free friends difficult
soufflé a French, baked egg-based dish made with egg yolks and beaten egg whites dinner entree or a dessert - you decide medium
tempering heating, cooling, and agitating chocolate refines the appearance and mouthfeel of chocolate difficult
silpat nonstick silicone mat that can be used for baking prevents things from sticking and is reusable easy
