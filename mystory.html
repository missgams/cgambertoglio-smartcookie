How it all started.

As a child, I enjoyed baking cookies and homemade bread with my grandma. In college, I would doctor box mixes for my roommates and friends, to their enjoyment upon late night returns. When I started teaching, I realized the box mixes weren’t going to cut it as the staff that baked used recipes from scratch. I started trying out more recipes from scratch and enjoyed sharing my attempts with family, friends and coworkers. The name of the website is a nod to my classroom theme, a class full of smart cookies that whip up something delicious monthly. In 2011, I started making cake pops and those became really popular. I was making them for weddings, showers, birthday parties, you name it. That trend slowly faded though and I started baking other things that interested me. Eventually, I started making more cupcakes and then layer cakes. I’m still learning a lot, but it’s fun to make custom cakes that are so well-received by their recipient. Hope I can bring a little happiness to you with a custom cake or bake!
